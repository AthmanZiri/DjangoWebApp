About sisiHub

We are the home of young developers, makers, creatives and entrepreneurs in Kisumu, Kenya.We promote innovation, collaboration and knowledge-sharing. Together with a community of experts we host tech events, workshops, meetups and hackathons focused on new technologies that solve real problems and promote better living.

Mentorship

Our approach to customer support encompasses every aspect of our agency, delivering outstanding results from start-ups to super-brands. Teams in the sisiHub Startup Program are given access to a strong local network of mentors and entrepreneurs.

Flexible Workspace

The sisiHub network enables members to work smarter, develop faster and increase their chances of startup success.

Support & Co-Working

Startups are provided with key resources necessary to develop their products. During the incubation, teams have our coworking space at their disposal 24/7
